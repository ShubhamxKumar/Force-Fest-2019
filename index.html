Principal's Message

Computer education in school plays an important role in students career development. The effective use of Technology in Education has changed the face of education and it has created more educational opportunities. To ensure that the youth remain on the cutting edge of technological advancements, Apeejay School , Sheikh Sarai has strived to promote technological development and understanding among students who shall be the leaders and innovators of the future. To help with this endeavour, the Force Fest- Forum Radical Computer Experts, a Computer Club of Apeejay School was founded in 2001. Forum Of Radical Computer Experts is an initiative which was started by Mr.Aditya Berlia and greatly supported by Dr. Stya Paul around two decades ago. This group of people not only work hard to be abreast of the innovation and changes in technology but it also makes sure that the students are able to compete with other schools. At last, our school motto 'Soaring High is My Nature' provides impetus to each member and we all enthusiastically work together towards it.
